Saharas' Beauty

The largest hot desert in the world, it divides the continent from east to west. It covers vast areas and extends over the territory of ten countries: Mali, Mauritania, Niger, Chad, Sudan, Algeria, Tunisia, Morocco, Libya, and Egypt, as well as the disputed territory of Western Sahara.
